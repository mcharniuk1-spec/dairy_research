# Final V10 Model and Thesis Reliability Audit

## Fact

- The main empirical base is the cleaned observed national monthly data: farm-gate raw milk, processor-level industrial producer prices, and official consumer prices.
- The strongest H1 price-link evidence is FarmGate -> Processor for drinking milk, hard cheese, kefir, and sour cream.
- The strongest H1 adjustment evidence is narrower: hard cheese has the clearest price-link and adjustment signal; drinking milk and sour cream have strong price links but weak or slow adjustment; kefir has a weak adjustment sign and is downgraded to supporting.
- Butter and skimmed milk powder remain supporting because diagnostics and old-new validation are weaker.
- The strongest H2 official evidence is Processor -> Consumer for sour cream. Drinking milk has a strong slope but weak long-run diagnostics and is downgraded to supporting.
- ProZorro -> Consumer is useful but short and supporting only.
- Cleaned ProZorro -> Retail rows are weak in direct coefficient terms; old retail/archive rows include significant coefficients but have sign, magnitude, and intersection risks.

## Interpretation

The thesis should not claim that every significant slope is proof of market power. The final interpretation should separate three layers:

1. **Price-link evidence:** prices move together in a statistically and economically meaningful way.
2. **Adjustment evidence:** disequilibria are corrected with a meaningful speed and sign.
3. **Market-power interpretation:** the price-link and adjustment pattern is consistent with bargaining asymmetry after product mapping, data quality, and institutional context are considered.
